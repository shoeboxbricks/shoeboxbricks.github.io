About

Originally a small gaming channel under the handle “pinkshoebox”, Shoebox Bricks is a Lego YouTube project initiated in 2022 by an ambitious Canadian creator. The channel is dedicated to delivering high quality videos documenting and showcasing various Lego builds, for the purpose of entertainment.

Pinkshoebox

“pinkshoebox” existed as a gaming channel from October 2020 to October 2022, uploading gaming content. The channel boasted a wide variety of Minecraft videos, including emotional montages, goofy Hypixel Bedwars, music video parodies, and horror let’s plays, to name a few. It had amassed a whopping 42 subscribers before its first Lego video in Oct. ’22.
